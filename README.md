# Privacy Policy for OpenClaw-Bot

Last updated: July 2026

Thank you for using OpenClaw-Bot ("the Bot"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and process information when you use the Bot in your Discord server.

### 1. Data We Collect
The Bot may collect and process the following information required to function properly:
- User IDs and Server (Guild) IDs
- Messages sent directly to the Bot or in channels where the Bot has access (only when the Bot is mentioned or triggered)

### 2. How We Use Your Data
We use the collected data solely for the following purposes:
- To generate responses using AI text/image generation APIs.
- To improve the Bot's functionality and performance.

### 3. Data Sharing and Third-Party APIs
To provide AI responses, the data you send to OpenClaw-Bot is forwarded to third-party AI service providers (such as OpenAI, Anthropic, or similar platforms). We do not share your data with any other third parties, and we do not sell your personal information.

### 4. Data Retention and Storage
We do not store your message history on our servers long-term. Messages are processed in real-time and immediately deleted after the AI response is generated, or kept temporarily for short-term conversation context.

### 5. Your Rights and Data Deletion
If you wish to request the deletion of any temporary data associated with your User ID or Server ID, please contact the developer.
